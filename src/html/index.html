hello.

I'm lisa, a software developer from Florida.

I design and create
clean, responsive, and
user friendly applications.

I am experienced in the full life cycle of software development and I enjoy everything from design, to front end and back end development.

My goal for every project is to create a product that is seamless for the user and built to scale for future features. I am knowledgeable in a variety of current tech stacks, and I'm always looking to become a better developer. Feel free to check out my LinkedIn or reach out for more details.

When I'm not in front of a screen, I love to take road trips and eat delicious food (I can't recommend Pappy's Smokehouse in St. Louis enough). My other interests include hiking, kayaking, and reading.
