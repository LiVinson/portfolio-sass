hello.

I'm lisa, a software developer from Florida.

I create clean, responsive, and user friendly applications.

I am experienced in the full life cycle of software development and I enjoy everything from design, to front end and back end development. My goal for every project is to create a product that is seamless for the user and built to scale for future features. I am knowledgeable in a variety of current tech stacks, and I'm always looking to become a better developer.

When I'm not in front of a screen, I love to take road trips, eat delicious food (I can't recommend Pappy's Smokehouse in St. Louis enough!), hike, kayak, and read.
